A detailed account of my academic journey and learning experiences.

Bishop Cotton School, Shimla (Till Grade 7)

My early schooling years were spent at Bishop Cotton School in Shimla, one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions in India. The environment here was a blend of academic rigor and holistic development. Surrounded by the scenic hills of Himachal Pradesh, I learned discipline, resilience, and the importance of balancing academics with extracurricular activities. The school emphasized character building, teamwork, and leadership, values that continue to shape my personality today.

Cambrian Hall, Dehradun (Grade 8)

After Bishop Cotton, I continued my journey at Cambrian Hall in Dehradun. This transition exposed me to a new environment and diverse peer groups. Cambrian Hall emphasized creativity and communication, encouraging students to participate in debates, cultural programs, and sports. Here, I strengthened my academic foundation while also learning adaptability — the ability to thrive in different settings and embrace change positively.

The Oasis School, Dehradun (Grades 9–10)

My high school years at The Oasis were pivotal. The curriculum was designed to prepare students for board examinations, and the focus was on analytical thinking and problem‑solving. I developed a strong interest in mathematics and science, subjects that later guided me toward data analysis and technology. The Oasis also encouraged extracurricular involvement, and I actively participated in sports and group projects, which taught me teamwork and perseverance.

DPSG Ghaziabad (Grades 11–12)

For my senior secondary education, I studied at Delhi Public School Ghaziabad (DPSG). These years were crucial as they prepared me for higher education and career choices. DPSG offered a rigorous academic environment with a strong emphasis on STEM subjects, business studies, and communication skills. I honed my analytical abilities, participated in presentations, and learned how to manage academic pressure effectively. The exposure to diverse subjects and projects here laid the groundwork for my future in data science and web development.

NMIMS University, Mumbai (Graduation)

Currently, I am pursuing my graduation at NMIMS University in Mumbai, specializing in MBA Tech Data Science. NMIMS has provided me with a platform to integrate business knowledge with technical expertise. Courses in data engineering, SQL, Power BI, machine learning, and web development have expanded my skill set, while projects and hackathons have given me practical exposure. The vibrant academic culture at NMIMS, combined with Mumbai’s dynamic environment, has helped me grow both professionally and personally. This stage of my education represents the culmination of my journey so far and the beginning of my aspirations to become a full‑stack developer and data scientist.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I aim to pursue advanced certifications and possibly postgraduate studies focusing on artificial intelligence, business analytics, and emerging technologies like Web3. My education journey — from Bishop Cotton to NMIMS — has taught me resilience, adaptability, and the importance of continuous learning. I see education not as a destination but as a lifelong process, and I am committed to evolving with every new challenge and opportunity.
